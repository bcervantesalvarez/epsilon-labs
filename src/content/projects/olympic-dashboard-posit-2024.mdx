## Notes from the archive

Multi-panel Quarto dashboard with `plotly` charts and `gt` tables. Useful as a reference for anyone who wants the look and feel of a dashboard without the deployment overhead of a reactive Shiny app. Built in a weekend as a demonstration piece.
